The Winningest Cities in America

Headline One

Since 1870, 311 distinct professional sports franchises have existed in North America1. In 2017, 1917 NCAA Div I teams represented 352 colleges and universities in women's basketball, soccer, and volleyball, and men's football, basketball and baseball2. In all, these teams have represented 154 cities across Canada and the United States and have competed for 1000 titles since 1870.

Here, I look at the last 148 years of sport to find North America's Titletowns — its winningest cities, the places which have known the most athletic success, the capitals of championship.
